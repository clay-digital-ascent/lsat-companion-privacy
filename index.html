Privacy Policy for LSAT Study Companion Chrome Extension

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the LSAT Study Companion Chrome extension ("the Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data Collection

The Extension collects and processes the following types of data:

  • LSAT Educational Content: When you use the Extension on supported LSAT preparation websites (including 7Sage, LSAT Demon, and Khan Academy), the Extension reads LSAT question text, answer choices, reading passages, and explanatory content solely for the purpose of providing AI-powered tutoring and explanations.
  • Study Progress Data: The Extension stores your interaction history locally in your browser, including which questions you have attempted, your accuracy rates, and time spent on different question types. This data is used to track your progress and identify areas for improvement.
  • User Preferences: Settings such as theme preference (light/dark mode), notification preferences, and study mode selections are stored locally in your browser.
  • Chat History: Your conversations with the AI tutor are stored locally to maintain context during study sessions.

2. Data Usage

  • AI Processing: LSAT question content is sent to AI service providers (OpenAI GPT models and/or Google Gemini) exclusively to generate educational explanations, answer rationales, and study guidance. No personally identifiable information is included in these API requests.
  • Progress Tracking: Study progress data is analyzed locally to provide personalized recommendations and identify patterns in your LSAT preparation.
  • Extension Functionality: User preferences are used to customize your experience with the Extension.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API (chrome.storage.local).
  • No data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • You can delete all stored data at any time by uninstalling the Extension or clearing your browser data for extensions.
  • LSAT question content sent to AI APIs is not retained by the AI providers beyond the duration needed to generate a response (as per their respective privacy policies).

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • LSAT question content is shared only with AI service providers (OpenAI and Google) strictly for the purpose of generating educational explanations.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Permissions Justification

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To temporarily access the current tab when you activate the Extension, allowing it to read LSAT question content for analysis.
  • Host Permissions (7sage.com, lsatdemon.com, khanacademy.org): To extract LSAT educational content from supported preparation websites.
  • sidePanel: To provide the AI tutoring interface in a side panel without navigating away from study materials.
  • storage: To save your preferences, study progress, and chat history locally in your browser.
  • scripting: To inject content scripts that extract LSAT question text from supported websites.
  • tabs: To detect when you are on LSAT preparation websites and activate the Extension appropriately.

6. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
